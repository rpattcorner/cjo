How I Work

I work in blocks of three hours at a time.

Daily Money Management clients typically work with me weekly or biweekly.

Some organizing clients need me every week, some once or twice a month, some two or three times a year.

I can also do a one-time assessment on your entire home and leave you with detailed notes and advice on how to proceed on your own.

I can also shop for organizing and office supplies for you, after we've gotten through the sorting and purging stages of organizing.
